For this first project I will start with a simple remote. This remote is a IR remote to control things by Infrared. 
This remote is based on a raspberry pi Pico, a IR receiver and emitter led, a 8 digit display and 3 buttons.
Also, you will need a transistor and resistors: 8 of 220 ohms and 1 of 10 ohms.
